EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT TRANSMISSION FESTIVAL MALAYSIA.
EVENT INFO

Name : TRANSMISSION FESTIVAL 2025

Date : 19th October 2025

Venue : Helipad @ Sepang International Circuit, Sepang F1 Circuit

 

HAVE FUN RESPONSIBLY

  • Take care of your friends and people around you.
  • Seek first aid if you or any of your friends are feeling unwell.
  • Stay hydrated and don’t forget to eat.
  • Don’t overdo it. Everyone has their limit, be responsible.
  • Take it easy and make sure to take breaks for rest.
  • Travel home safely with friends, use public transportation.

 

WHAT ARE THE OPENING HOURS?

The event hours are from 3:00 PM to 12:00 AM.

 

WHAT IS THE MINIMUM AGE TO ACCESS THE EVENT?

Attendees must be 18 years old and above to enter.

WHAT IS THE LINE UP AND THE TIMETABLE?

The line-up: Aly & Fila, Ben Nicky pres. Emotional Havoc, Boris Foong, Chukies & Whackboi, Daxson, Digital Culture, Digital Rush B2B Gio Dytto, Driftmoon, Markus Schulz (Rabbit Hole Set), MisterArifin.

Timetable To Be Announced (TBA). Please check back closer to the event date for updates.

 

WHAT IS THE SOUND LEVEL?

The sound level is between 100 and 105 db. It’s strongly advised to wear earplugs.

WILL MERCHANDISE BE AVAILABLE AT THE EVENT?

Yes, an selection of merchandise will be available for purchase on the event day.

 

WHAT IS NOT ALLOWED TO BRING IN OR WEAR AT THE EVENT?

Specific restrictions on items not allowed to bring in or wear will be communicated closer to the event date. Please check for updates on the official website or social media channels.

VENUE
WHAT IS THE ADDRESS OF THE VENUE?

The venue address is Sepang International Circuit, Sepang F1 Circuit Jalan Pekeliling, 64000 KLIA, Selangor Darul Ehsan, Malaysia.

HOW DO I GET TO THE VENUE?

You can get to the venue by car or e-hailing services.

WHICH ENTRANCE CAN I USE TO ENTER THE EVENT?

TBA.

IS THERE A BARRIER-FREE ENTRANCE FOR PEOPLE WITH DISABILITIES?

No, there is no barrier-free entrance available.

WHAT IF I USE MEDICATION AND/OR HAVE AN ALLERGY?

If you require medication or have allergies, please ensure you carry necessary documentation and inform event staff if you need assistance.

ARE THERE CLOAKROOMS OR LOCKERS AVAILABLE?

No, there are no cloakrooms or lockers available at the venue.

CAN I BRING MY PHOTO/VIDEO CAMERA WITH ME?

Yes, you can bring your mobile or compact digital photo/video camera with you. Professional photo/video cameras (with interchangeable lenses) are only allowed for people who have received a special photo pass accreditation.

 

 

I AM A PHOTOGRAPHER/JOURNALIST, HOW DO I GET A PRESS CARD?

Information regarding press accreditation for photographers and journalists will be provided closer to the event date. Please check the official website for details on how to apply for a press card.

HOW DO I PAY AT THE VENUE?

There will be a box office at the venue, and payments will be cashless.

 

AM I ALLOWED TO SMOKE IN THE VENUE?

Yes, smoking is allowed in the venue.

VISITING REGULATIONS

Specific visiting regulations for the event will be communicated closer to the date. Please stay updated through official channels for any rules or guidelines you need to follow.
